Hello!

Hello, my name is Emanuele Franchi and I am a programmer!

I first started learning about programming and computer science when I started studying computer engineering at university, in Pavia, in 2016. Amongst the many first semester courses, there was one in particular that caught my attention. It was a course that taught the basics of how a computer works, on a low level perspective. After that I learnt my first programming language: C. There, I also took courses on operating systems, data bases and object oriented programming. Course after course, I started to realize my deep appreciation for computer science as a subject. This led me to move to another university, in Turin, to start my computer science degree, in 2018. The courses I had the most fun with talked about algorithms and data structures, automata theory and compilers, formal methods, functional programming and lambda calculus.

After three beautiful years of studying, in April 2022, I finally graduated getting the highest score, 110L, doing a lab thesis on the Vulkan graphics API.
